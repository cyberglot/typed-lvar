Terms ($\tm{\ell}$, $\tm{M}$, $\tm{N}$) are defined by the following grammar:
\[
  \begin{array}{lrll}
  \tm{\ell}, \tm{M}, \tm{N}
  & \coloneqq & \tm{x}
     \sep        \tm{\lambda x.M}
     \sep        \tm{M\;N}   \\
  & \sep      & \tm{\ell}
      \sep        \tm{d}
       \sep        \tmJ
       \sep       \tm{\iota}
  \\
  \\
   \tm{K}
  & \coloneqq & \tm{\lvarget}
    \sep        \tm{\lvarput}
    \sep        \tm{return}
    \sep        \tm{withLVar}
\end{array}
\]
